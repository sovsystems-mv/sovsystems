# Video Caption Generator

Transform video transcripts into high-conversion titles and captions for short-form platforms.

## When to Use

- You have a video transcript or topic summary
- You need titles + captions for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, or TikTok
- Content is AI/marketing/business focused

## Required Input

Provide ONE of these:
1. Video transcript (raw or cleaned)
2. Topic summary with key points
3. Video file reference (I'll ask for the core message)

## Brand Voice Rules

| Principle | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| Authority | Lead with proof and results. No "shiny object syndrome." |
| Contrarian | Challenge AI hype. "Speed to Clarity" over "Speed to Market." |
| Systems-First | AI amplifies human judgment. Build scalable systems. |
| Human Filter | AI handles volume; human taste is the moat. |

## Output Format

For each video, I generate:

### 1. Three Title Options

| Type | Angle | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Contrarian** | Challenges common belief | "AI Slop is Killing Your Brand" |
| **Authority** | Expert positioning | "The New AI Trust Signals" |
| **Outcome** | Direct benefit/ROI | "The Only AI Moat Left" |

### 2. Caption (Under 75 Words)

Structure:
- **Hook:** Punchy opener identifying problem or market shift
- **Meat:** 2-3 sentences on execution and ROI
- **CTA:** Bold question forcing self-evaluation

### 3. Hashtags

Exactly 4 relevant hashtags per caption.

## Quality Checklist

Before delivering, I verify:
- [ ] Titles under 60 characters
- [ ] Caption under 75 words
- [ ] Hook grabs attention in first sentence
- [ ] CTA is a question (not a command)
- [ ] No banned words (delve, unleash, game-changer, etc.)
- [ ] Passes the "So What?" test—real value, not hype
- [ ] Exactly 4 hashtags
